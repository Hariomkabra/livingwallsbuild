Modular Kitchen

How do I buy a modular kitchen? ▼
You can buy a modular kitchen by consulting with a designer, selecting a layout, choosing materials, and placing an order with a supplier.
What is the best modular kitchen design? ▼
The best design depends on your space; L-shaped kitchens are great for small spaces, while U-shaped ones suit larger areas.
How can I accessorize my kitchen to increase storage? ▼
Use pull-out drawers, corner units, and overhead cabinets to maximize storage in your kitchen.
What is the best material for a modular kitchen? ▼
Plywood with a laminate finish is often recommended for durability and aesthetics.
Is PVC good for a modular kitchen? ▼
PVC is affordable and water-resistant but less durable than plywood or MDF for long-term use.
What is the best material for kitchen cabinets? ▼
Plywood or MDF with a laminate or acrylic finish offers a good balance of durability and style.
What accessories should I include in a modular kitchen? ▼
Include pull-out organizers, cutlery trays, corner units, and built-in appliances for functionality.
What are the units of a modular kitchen? ▼
Units include base cabinets, wall cabinets, tall units, and accessories like drawers and shelves.
How do I design a modular kitchen on a budget? ▼
Opt for simple designs, use cost-effective materials like laminates, and prioritize essential units.
What does a modular kitchen cost? ▼
Costs vary widely based on size, materials, and accessories, typically ranging from $1,500 to $10,000 or more.
What appliances does a modular kitchen include/accommodate? ▼
It can accommodate ovens, microwaves, dishwashers, and refrigerators, with built-in slots for seamless integration.
What is the minimum cost of a modular kitchen? ▼
A basic modular kitchen can start at around $1,500, depending on size and materials.

Wardrobe

How do I choose a wardrobe? ▼
Consider your storage needs, available space, and style preferences when choosing a wardrobe.
What are the types of wardrobe? ▼
The types of wardrobe are hinged, sliding and armadio wardrobes.
  • Hinged Wardrobe
  • Sliding Wardrobe
  • Armadio Wardrobe
    • Which material is best for wardrobes? ▼
    The best material for wardrobes is HDHMR (high-density-high-moisture resistant) due to its water resistance and termite-free nature.
    How do I pick the right wardrobe material? ▼
    In order to pick the right wardrobe material, you must keep the following tips in mind :
  • PLPB - Eco-friendly & pocket-friendly
  • HDHMR - Eco-friendly & termite-free.
  • Plywood - Sturdy & long-lasting, These are some of the best-selling materials for wardrobes in the modular industry.
    • What is the best finish for a wardrobe? ▼
    The best finish options for wardrobes are high-gloss laminate and acrylic as they are easy to clean and maintain.
    What are the best design ideas for a wardrobe? ▼
    The best wardrobe designs are hinged, sliding and armadio wardrobes.
  • Hinged wardrobe - These are widely used, cost-effective and fit corners well
  • Sliding wardrobe - Modern, space-saving as the doors don't wing out
  • Floor to ceiling sliding wardrobe (Armadio) - This is a premium finish if you do not like separate loft shutters
    • Is MDF good for wardrobes?▼
    Yes, MDF is one of the best materials for wardrobes. It is cost-effective and eco-friendly as it involves a recycling process.

    Space Saving Furniture

    What is space-saving furniture? ▼
    Space-saving furniture is designed to maximize small spaces, like foldable tables or wall-mounted desks.
    How much room do you need for a Murphy bed? ▼
    For a murphy bed, you require a minimum of 8x9 sq.ft for a queen size, while single murphy beds can fit in smaller spaces.
    What is the average cost of a Murphy bed? ▼
    The average cost of a Murphy bed (single horizontal bed) is about Rs 54,000. It depends on the material and the type of bed.
    Are Murphy beds a good idea? ▼
    Yes, wall (murphy) beds are great for small spaces. In the bedroom, it doubles up as furniture that can be used for sleep and for study/work; it converts your bedroom to a multi-functional room.

    TV Unit

    What are the best TV unit designs? ▼
    Wall-mounted units with storage or minimalist designs work well for modern homes.
    What are the best TV unit designs? ▼
    Wall-mounted units with storage or minimalist designs work well for modern homes.
    How can I add storage to my TV unit? ▼
    Incorporate shelves, drawers, or cabinets below or beside the TV unit to maximize storage space.
    What is the average price of an TV unit? ▼
    The average price of a TV unit can range from Rs 10,000 to Rs 50,000, depending on material, design, and brand.
    Which wood is best for TV unit? ▼
    Teak and walnut are popular choices for TV units due to their durability and aesthetic appeal.
    What are the types of material used for TV units? ▼
    Common materials include wood (teak, oak, walnut), engineered wood, metal, glass, and MDF (medium-density fiberboard).
    What is the average size TV for living room? ▼
    For a typical living room, a 43 to 55-inch TV is ideal, depending on the viewing distance.
    Is it better to wall mount a TV or put it on a stand? ▼
    Wall mounting saves space and offers a sleek look, while a stand provides flexibility and easier access to ports.

    Puja Unit

    How do I design a puja unit? ▼
    Use a small, dedicated space with shelves and a traditional design for a puja unit.
    What are the best puja unit designs? ▼
    Compact wall-mounted or corner puja units with intricate carvings and shelves for idols are ideal for modern homes.
    What is the cost of puja units based on size? ▼
    Small puja units may cost around Rs 5,000 to Rs 15,000, while larger ones can range from Rs 20,000 to Rs 50,000, depending on size and craftsmanship.
    How can I choose the best material for puja units? ▼
    Opt for durable materials like teak or rosewood for a traditional look, or engineered wood for a budget-friendly, modern option.

    Study Unit

    What is a study unit? ▼
    A study unit is a compact workspace with a desk, shelves, and storage for books and supplies.
    What is the best furniture for a home office? ▼
    A sturdy desk, ergonomic chair, and storage units like shelves or cabinets are ideal for a home office.
    How to design an effective study unit? ▼
    Prioritize a well-lit space, a comfortable desk and chair, and organize with shelves for books and supplies.
    How much does it cost for a home office? ▼
    A basic home office setup can cost between Rs 20,000 to Rs 50,000, depending on furniture quality and size.
    How to design a study unit on a small budget? ▼
    Use affordable materials like MDF, opt for a foldable desk, and add DIY shelves for storage.
    What is the recommended material for a study unit? ▼
    Engineered wood or MDF is recommended for durability and cost-effectiveness; solid wood like teak adds elegance.
    What are the types of study unit finish? ▼
    Common finishes include laminate, veneer, matte, glossy, and natural wood polish for a premium look.
    How do I add storage to a study unit? ▼
    Incorporate wall-mounted shelves, under-desk drawers, or a cabinet to keep books and supplies organized.

    Interior Design

    How do I start with interior design? ▼
    Start by defining your style, setting a budget, and consulting with a professional designer.
    What are the best interior design ideas for a living room? ▼
    Opt for a cohesive color scheme, use multifunctional furniture, add statement lighting, and incorporate textures like rugs and throw pillows to create a warm, inviting space.
    What are the best interior design ideas for a bedroom? ▼
    Focus on calming colors, invest in a comfortable bed with quality bedding, use layered lighting, and add personal touches like artwork or plants.
    What is the average size bedroom? ▼
    The average bedroom size in the U.S. is about 11x12 feet (132 square feet), though this varies based on the home's age and location.
    What size bed can fit in a 10x10 room? ▼
    A 10x10 room can comfortably fit a twin or full-size bed, leaving space for a small nightstand and dresser. A queen might fit but will feel cramped.
    What are the best interior design ideas for a kids bedroom? ▼
    Use bright, playful colors, incorporate storage solutions like shelves and bins, add a themed bed or mural, and ensure the space is safe with rounded furniture edges.
    How do I design my kid's bedroom? ▼
    Involve your child in choosing a theme, prioritize safety and functionality, create zones for sleep, play, and study, and use durable, easy-to-clean materials.
