Frequently Asked Questions

Planning and Budgeting

We know the fear of unexpected costs can be stressful. That’s why we provide a detailed budget breakdown upfront, including a contingency plan for surprises. You’ll receive weekly financial updates, so you’re always in control and can plan with confidence.

Uncertainty about timelines can make anyone anxious. We provide a clear timeline during the planning phase—row houses typically take 6-12 months, while apartments may take 18-24 months. We also send regular progress updates, so you always know what to expect.

We get it—delays can feel frustrating. We build buffer time into our schedules for weather or other unforeseen issues. If delays happen, we’ll communicate a revised timeline immediately and ensure resources are ready to resume work, keeping your project on track.

We understand financial constraints can be daunting. We offer flexible payment plans, breaking the cost into manageable milestones. During our initial consultation, we’ll work with you to create a payment schedule that fits your budget, ensuring you can move forward without stress.

Material cost changes can be unsettling. We lock in prices for key materials at the start of your project whenever possible. If unexpected fluctuations occur, we’ll discuss alternatives with you and adjust the budget transparently, ensuring no surprises.

Design and Customization

It’s normal to evolve your vision as the project takes shape. We’ll assess the impact of changes on the timeline and budget, then provide a clear plan to integrate them seamlessly. Your dream home should reflect your latest ideas without stress.

Absolutely! We offer full customization options, from layouts to finishes. Our design team will work with you to create a space that feels uniquely yours, ensuring every detail aligns with your personal style and needs.

We understand the disappointment of unfeasible ideas. Our team will guide you through what’s possible, offering creative alternatives that align with your vision while meeting structural and regulatory requirements. You’ll feel heard and inspired.

Yes, sustainability is a priority for many clients! We can incorporate eco-friendly features like solar panels, energy-efficient windows, and sustainable materials. We’ll collaborate with you to design a home that’s both stylish and environmentally responsible.

It’s okay to feel unsure—we’re here to help you discover your style. Our design team will present you with mood boards, 3D renderings, and material samples to explore options. We’ll guide you through the process until you’re confident in your choices.

Construction Process

We understand the need for peace of mind during construction. You’ll receive weekly progress reports with photos and updates, plus access to a dedicated project manager for any questions. We keep you in the loop every step of the way.

Quality concerns can be nerve-wracking. We conduct rigorous inspections at every stage to ensure top standards. If an issue arises, we address it immediately at no extra cost, ensuring your home meets the highest quality benchmarks.

Miscommunication can cause unnecessary stress. We assign a dedicated project manager as your single point of contact, and we use 3D visualizations to ensure your vision is clear. Regular check-ins keep everything aligned with your expectations.

We encourage site visits so you can see your dream home come to life! We’ll schedule safe, guided visits at key milestones, ensuring you feel connected to the process while adhering to safety protocols.

Disputes can be unsettling, but you won’t have to worry. We manage all subcontractors directly and have strict contracts in place to ensure accountability. If issues arise, we handle them promptly to keep your project on track.

Trust and Decision-Making

Trust is everything in a project this personal. Explore our portfolio of completed projects—societies, apartments, and row houses—and read client testimonials. We also offer a free consultation to answer your questions and build confidence in our partnership.

Decision fatigue is real, and we’re here to help. Our team guides you through each choice with expert advice, from layouts to materials, ensuring you feel supported. We break decisions into manageable steps, so you can focus on the excitement of building your home.

We know you have options, and we’re here to earn your trust. At Living Walls, we combine 20+ years of experience with a client-first approach. Our transparent pricing, regular updates, and commitment to quality set us apart. Let’s build your dream home together—schedule a free consultation today!

It’s natural to want your home to stand the test of time. We use only high-quality materials and adhere to strict building codes. Plus, we offer a warranty on our work, so you can have peace of mind knowing your home is built to last.

Investing in a home is a big decision, and we’re here to ensure it’s the right one. We provide market insights on property value trends in your area and design homes that enhance resale value. Our goal is to make your investment both emotionally and financially rewarding.
