Begin your home construction journey by booking with us. You can set-up a meeting using our website, give us a call, or contact us via WhatsApp.

Frequently Asked Question

What are services BUILDHOOD provides?

BUILDHOOD offers a range of construction services, including residential and commercial building, renovations, and interior design.

Why should you choose BUILDHOOD?

BUILDHOOD stands out for its commitment to quality, on-time delivery, and customer satisfaction, backed by years of industry experience.

Do you charge separately for Designs?

No, design services are typically included in our project packages, but it depends on the scope of work.

Do you help with government approvals?

Yes, we assist clients with obtaining necessary government approvals and permits for their projects.

How do you assure on-time completion of the project?

We follow a strict timeline, use project management tools, and maintain clear communication to ensure timely completion.

How do you monitor the work quality?

We conduct regular inspections, use quality materials, and follow industry standards to ensure high-quality work.

How is BUILDHOOD payment process?

We follow a milestone-based payment process, with payments due at key stages of the project, ensuring transparency.

Whom to contact during work?

You’ll have a dedicated project manager as your point of contact throughout the project.

Does the Client need to follow up the work regularly?

Not necessarily. We provide regular updates, but clients are welcome to check in as needed.

How does the Client monitor the progress of the project?

Clients can monitor progress through regular updates, site visits, and a project management portal if applicable.
