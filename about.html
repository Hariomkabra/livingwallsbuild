About Us

Established in Bangalore, (Karnataka) India in the year 2018, we “Livingwalls Projects Pvt Ltd are engaged in providing various kind of civil construction service that include Rainwater Harvesting, Electrical Works Civil Construction work, Roads, Layouts & Industrial Sheeds Designing, Fabrication and Laying Service, Interiors and Exteriors services. These are offered as per the requirement of our esteemed clients.


We use high quality raw material throughout our service in every field as we are one of the renowned quality centric organization and quality is our prior aim. We procure the raw material from quality driven organization that is checked at our quality inspection unit as to provide unique quality service to our esteemed clients. We offer these services after complete planning and designing of the project.
